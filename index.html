Picks of the day:

Butter chicken

Butter chicken is a traditional Indian food also known as murgh makhani, it's a type of curry made of chicken with spiced tomato and butter with a mixture of lemon juice and dahi(yogurt)

Chole bhature

Chole bhature is a popular north India dish it is a combination of chana masala(spicy white chickpeas) and and bhatura a deep fried bread made from maida

Shahi paneer

Shahi paneer or Paneer Rajwadi is a preparation of chhena or paneer, native to the Indian subcontinent, consisting of a thick gravy of cream, tomatoes and Indian spices.
