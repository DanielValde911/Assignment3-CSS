About Us

Mama's Food is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving delicious Indian cuisine for over 20 years. Our menu features a variety of appetizers, entrees, non-veg curries, and desserts that are made with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes. We take pride in providing our customers with a warm and welcoming dining experience and look forward to serving you soon.
